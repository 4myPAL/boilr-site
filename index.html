What


Boilr is an Android app which monitors cryptocurrencies and bullion prices and triggers alarms according to user rules.


It supports 2 types of alarms:

Price Hit
Triggered when price crosses an upper or lower bound.

Price Change
Triggered when price changes more than a given amount (in currency or percentage) in a specified time frame (e.g. 1 day).


Alarm sound and vibration are configurable globally and for each alarm in particular.


Lists of exchanges, pairs and alarms are fully searchable allowing a quick alarm setup and configuration.


Market data is retrieved directly from the exchanges using Wi-Fi or Mobile Data (if allowed). Boilr supports all the exchanges available on libdynticker with all their traded pairs.

If you rotate your phone to horizontal orientation, Boilr will display its information in greater detail.
